Pins:

GND - Ground pin
VCC - power supply pin, 5V
SDA - I2C data pin 
SCL - I2C clock pin 
I2C1
      SDA – PB7 or PB9
      SCL – PB6 or PB8
I2C2
      SDA – PB11
      SCL – PB10

A0, A1, A2 - adresy jednotlivych i2c deviceov, kedze su vsetky nastavene na jednicku tak adresa by mala byt 0x3F (Ide o chip PFC8754A)
